# Coffee-Sales-Dashboard
## Project Overview

This Excel-based dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee sales performance across multiple years (2019–2022). It is designed to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence, allowing stakeholders to track revenue trends, identify high-value customers, and understand regional market penetration.


## Key KPIs Addressed
### The dashboard solves for several critical business metrics:

### Revenue Growth Trends: Monitored via the "Total Sales Over Time" line chart, segmented by coffee bean type (Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, and Robusta).

### Customer Retention & Value: Identifies the "Top 5 Customers" to help personalize marketing efforts or loyalty rewards.

### Geographic Market Share: Breaks down sales volume by country (United States, Ireland, United Kingdom) to pinpoint dominant markets.

### Product Performance: Analyzes which roast types (Light, Medium, Dark) and package sizes (0.2kg to 2.5kg) drive the most volume.

### Loyalty Impact: Evaluates the effectiveness of the loyalty card program on overall sales figures.

## Technical Process
### Data Cleaning & ETL: * Used XLOOKUP and INDEX/MATCH to merge customer and product data into a master sales sheet.

### Standardized date formats and handled missing values to ensure timeline accuracy.

### Data Modeling: * Created multiple Pivot Tables to aggregate data by date, bean type, country, and customer name.

## UI/UX Design:

### Implemented a dark-themed header with a high-contrast purple/lavender color palette for professional readability.

### Integrated Slicers and Timelines for interactive filtering by Roast Type, Size, Loyalty Card status, and specific time periods.

## Visualization:

### Utilized dynamic Line Charts and Bar Charts that update instantly based on slicer selections.

## Project Insights
### Product Dominance: Sales trends indicate specific bean types (e.g., Excelsa and Liberica) often experience sharp, seasonal spikes, suggesting potential for limited-time promotional offers.

### Market Concentration: The United States represents the primary revenue driver, significantly outperforming the Ireland and UK markets in the current dataset.

### Inventory Optimization: By analyzing sales by "Size," the dashboard reveals which packaging (e.g., 2.5kg vs. 0.5kg) is most popular, allowing for better stock management and reduced waste.

### Customer Loyalty: The dashboard highlights the revenue gap between loyalty card holders and non-holders, providing a clear case for expanding the loyalty program.
